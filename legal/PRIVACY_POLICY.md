## 1. Introduction

EGWallet ("we," "our," or "us") operates the EGWallet mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. By using EGWallet, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

When you register for and use EGWallet, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Email address** — used for account registration, authentication, and communication.
- **Username** — a unique handle you choose for peer-to-peer transfers.
- **Password** — stored in hashed form; we never store or have access to your plaintext password.

### 2.2 Financial Information

- **Wallet ID** — a unique identifier assigned to your digital wallet.
- **Transaction history** — records of all transfers sent and received, including amounts, currencies, timestamps, and counterparties.
- **Currency preferences** — your selected default currency.

### 2.3 Device and Usage Information

- **Device type and operating system** — used for compatibility and troubleshooting.
- **App usage data** — screens visited, features used, and interaction patterns to improve the user experience.
- **Crash reports and diagnostics** — collected to identify and resolve technical issues.

### 2.4 Biometric Data

- **Biometric authentication status** — we check whether biometric authentication (fingerprint or face recognition) is enabled on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your biometric data. All biometric processing occurs locally on your device.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

- **Account management** — to create and maintain your account, authenticate your identity, and enable account recovery.
- **Transaction processing** — to facilitate money transfers between users, maintain transaction records, and provide transaction receipts.
- **Security and fraud prevention** — to detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activity.
- **Communication** — to send you service-related notices, security alerts, and support responses.
- **App improvement** — to analyze usage patterns, diagnose technical issues, and improve App functionality and performance.
- **Legal compliance** — to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We retain your personal and financial data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

- **Encryption in transit** — all data transmitted between the App and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
- **Password hashing** — user passwords are salted and hashed using industry-standard algorithms (bcrypt). We never store plaintext passwords.
- **Token-based authentication** — secure JSON Web Tokens (JWT) are used for session management with automatic expiration.
- **Access controls** — access to user data is restricted to authorized systems and personnel on a need-to-know basis.
- **Rate limiting** — API endpoints are protected against brute-force and denial-of-service attacks.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
